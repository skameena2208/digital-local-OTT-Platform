OTT services or platforms “Over-The-Top”

OTT, or over-the-top, is a way of delivering content over the internet, instead of through satellite, cable, or broadcast TV. OTT platforms offer video content on a variety of devices, including traditional closed TVs. OTT content is typically on-demand, allowing users to watch what and when they want, but can also include live streaming.
